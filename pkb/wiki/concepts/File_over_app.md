# File over app

> A philosophy that prioritizes universal file formats (like Markdown) over proprietary application silos.

## 核心原则 (Core Principles)
1. **Interoperable (互操作性)**: Data should be accessible by any tool or CLI, not just one app.
2. **Durable (持久性)**: Files in universal formats (Markdown, images) outlast specific software companies.
3. **Local (本地所有)**: Your information stays on your computer, giving you full control and privacy.
4. **Agent-friendly (Agent 友好)**: AI Agents can apply the entire Unix toolkit over a collection of simple files.

## 🛠️ Tools of choice
- **Obsidian**: For viewing and manual management.
- **pkb CLI**: For AI-driven ingestion and querying.
- **Git**: For version control and historical research.
