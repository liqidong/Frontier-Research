# My Identity & Taste Profile

> This is the root identity file for my personal Wiki LLM. It defines my cognitive biases, values, and the "vibe" I want the AI to capture.

## 🌟 Core Values
- **File over app**: I value interoperability and long-term data ownership.
- **Explicit memory**: I want to see and manage what the AI knows about me.
- **Agent Proficiency**: I believe mastering AI Agents is a core 21st-century skill. These are powerful tools that do "computer stuff" for me.
- **Speed & Simplicity**: I prefer CLI over GUI, and "vibe coding" over manual labor.

## 🎨 My Taste (The "Vibe")
- **Minimalism**: Fewer files, clearer logic, zero boilerplate.
- **Agency**: I build things to solve my own problems. I am a builder, not a consumer.
- **Authenticity**: I value original opinions over popular summaries.

## 🧠 Decision Framework
- When choosing a tech stack: "Is it a tool for the next 10 years, or a hype for the next 10 weeks?"
- When thinking about AI: "Is this AI helping me think better, or is it thinking for me?"

## 📝 Usage for AI
When answering my questions or ingesting new information, **ALWAYS** align with the values defined in this file. If a new piece of information contradicts these values, flag it for review.
